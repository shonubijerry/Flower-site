OUR STORY

XYZ years ago, anyone watching Mrs. Majekodunmi creating flower arrangements for her church on her balcony, never would have imagined that Newton & David Events as it stands today was what was budding (pun emphatically intended). Named for the company founder’s X & Y, the story & prominence of N&D has been a testament to the rewards of hard work, commitment to excellence & consistency over its XYZ years. What started as a floristry service in 19XY, grew into Nigeria’s leading event decorating company that serviced clients globally. N&DE went on and morphed even further into a full service event company that integrates spatial and experience design as well as management and production. The augmentation in our scope of work is a clear testament to Mrs. Majekodunmi’s //(or our) hard work and relentless dedication - traits that are part of the underlying current that continues to push N&D through interesting times, trials and trends.
