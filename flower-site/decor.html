DECOR

Our in-house design team of creatives masterfully conceptualize, design and build custom spaces, scenes and sets that essentially create immersive experiences for guests/attendees. Using our extensive stock and access to fabrics, flora, decorative props, custom-made items, lighting effects, fabricators and technicians, our design team creates beautifully impactful (beautiful and impactful) spaces for you and your guests.

Our design services include:

Artistic, digital and technical Installations for private and corporate/branding events, Venue transformation, Floral Artistry, Graphics and rendering of Event Design specifications, Accurate Screen to Space execution of design plans, Design and Printing services, Stage building, Custom Scenic builds– flats and 3D
