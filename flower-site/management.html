MANAGEMENT

Our exceptional management team should be your first call for creative and innovative design-led experience orchestration. Our project managers skillfully create and develop design-led immersive experiences for discerning private and corporate clients. We delight in offering our clients a fully integrated service that spans but isn’t limited to personalized concept development, venue design and fabrication, event management and production. This means that the intense detail-ridden work of making decisions surrounding décor, digital & technical installations and your event’s smooth orchestration is left to our experienced professionals who you can hold accountable, and collaborate with, if desired.

For corporate clients, our management team will work with your business to develop an Effective Experience Strategy that comprises of a series of events targeting growth in brand awareness, customer engagement and product launch & sales. (You might not be aware but//) According to The Event Marketing 2018 Benchmarks and Trends report (Bizzabo), 80% of marketers and executivesbelieve that strategically targeted events contribute significantly to an organization’s success in impressions and sales. Over time // So it works to our clients’ benefit that//, we have mastered the art of using a broad spectrum of branding events to create positive Customer Experiences (CX) on behalf of our clients.

Event MANAGEMENT scope includes –

Venue scouting and contract negotiations, Vendor options and contract negotiations, Event Experience Design and Orchestration – Event flow, talent booking, entertainment, Hiring on-site Event Coordinating staff – Ushers and Security. Graphics design & Printing of client’s branding, Invitation material design, printing and distribution (Digital or Hardcopy), Design and Printing Event communication materials – order of event booklets, menus, place cards,Reconciliation of vendor invoices and inventory, Destination management – accommodation, transportation and local staffing, Guest list management, Hospitality and logistics Support, Location Security & Emergency Care services, Audio-Visuals.
