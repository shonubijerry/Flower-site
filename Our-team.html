OUR TEAM

Our superpower is our team of uninhibited, enthusiastic, highly-skilled creatives and project managers that understand fully well that “great design is an important component of great experiences”.

Our event production team has a geek-level obsession with the art of spatial design and event production. We are constantly researching and curating design inspiration across various cultural, geographical, artistic and historical influences.

At Newton & David Events, we have a deep understanding of spatial design requirements and guest psychology in event planning.

While using design elements to create well decorated spaces, we are well aware of the psychology of design and understand that the real goal is to tell our client’s intended story. Our focus is using the full application event design and production to evoke a positively striking impression on attendees. The Newton & David Events team develops personalized concepts for our clients that are attentive and unique to the individual and their targeted message. We brainstorm, strategize, design, market and produce events with specific messaging to cohesively tell the desired story with the aim of propelling people and ideas.

Our team includes design, fabrication & production managers working alongside technicians who masterfully build custom sets to fabricate scenic and engaging spaces that optimize guests’, attendees’ and customers’ experiences.Our well-rounded, highly skilled design team includes:

EVENT DESIGNERS, INTERIOR DECORATORS (FOR SEASONAL/THEMED OFFICE OR HOME DÉCOR –Valentine’s, Christmas), EVENT DECORATORS, GRAPHIC DESIGNERS, FABRICATION MANAGERS, PRODUCTION MANAGERS AND TECHNICIANS, FLORISTS, TABLESCAPERS, ONSITE FABRICATION SUPERVISORS AND TECHNICIANS.

The event management arm of N&DE caters to individuals and companies that need event producers they can trust with orchestrating an excellent event holistically. Working with us grants you access to a team of meticulous, forward thinkers who are focused on making sure your event’s attendees have a positively memorable experience they appreciate you for. We consult and collaborate with established and emerging service providers that understand the relationship between design-led hospitality services and enriched sensorial experiences. One of the advantages of engaging our event management service is the access to our network of excellence-minded, professional service providers with whom we have cultivated strong working relationships. This is an important resource that ensures top quality service from the various vendors being managed and provides seamless flow of work for and during your event.

EVENT PROJECT MANAGERS, EVENT CO-ORDINATORS, STAGE & Talent MANAGERS, ONSITE STAFF SUPEPVISORS.
